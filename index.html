About

The Lodge at Grist Iron

The Lodge at Grist Iron Brewing Co. sits 6 miles north of Watkins Glen on a scenic 150-acre property overlooking Seneca Lake — centrally located on the Beer, Wine, and Cheese Trails and the perfect home base for a Finger Lakes adventure. It's the only lodging in the region with an onsite microbrewery, so house-brewed craft beer, wood-fired pizza, and smoked BBQ are just steps from your room.

Choose from nine recently renovated, simple-modern rooms — including select dog-friendly rooms — or bring the whole crew to the historic Farmhouse, which sleeps up to 16. Every stay includes a complimentary continental breakfast in a bright, airy common area with killer views of the lake, plus check-in beers to get things started. With 30+ wineries, breweries, distilleries, and restaurants within 10 miles, good times are always on tap.

Plan Your Stay

Tips for Guests

  • Book directly through gristironbrewing.com — weekends sell out well in advance, especially in summer and fall.
  • Two ways to stay: the Lodge is hotel-style with private rooms; the Farmhouse is a whole private house for groups up to 16.
  • Ask for an upper-floor room if you can — those balconies have the best Seneca Lake views and morning light.
  • Traveling with a dog? Request a dog-friendly room, and let them run at the fenced 6-acre dog park (yes, it has its own bar).
  • Use your complimentary check-in beers on the brewery patio, then grab a wood-fired pizza before heading back to your room.
  • Watkins Glen State Park is about 6 miles south — pair a morning gorge hike with an afternoon on the disc golf course.
